Everyone Deserves a Path Foward

Supporting individuals experiencing homelessness through access to shelter, food assistance, employment resources, and community support.

Path Forward connects people with local services that help meet immediate needs while creating opportunities for long-term stability and independence.

Our Mission

At Path Forward, our mission is to empower individuals experiencing homelessness by connecting them with essential resources, supportive services, and opportunities for growth.

We believe that stable housing, access to food, employment opportunities, and mental health support are fundamental building blocks for a stronger future. Through community partnerships and awareness initiatives, we work to help individuals navigate challenges and take meaningful steps toward stability.

Understanding the Challenge

Why Homelessness Awareness Matters Homelessness affects communities across the country and can impact people from all walks of life. Economic hardship, housing instability, health challenges, and unexpected life events can all contribute to an individual's experience with homelessness.
580,000+ People experience homelessness on a given night in the United States.
30%+ Of individuals experiencing homelessness report mental health challenges that require ongoing support.
Thousands Of community organizations work every day to provide shelter, food assistance, employment services, and crisis support.
Every Action Matters Volunteering, donating, and spreading awareness can help strengthen local support networks and create positive change.
